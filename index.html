Mahikeng, South Africa

Mahikeng is situated in the North West Province of South Africa and is a border town near South Africa's border with Botswana. The town serves as the seat of the North West Provincial Government and the North West Division of the High Court of South Africa. Mahikeng is also home to one of the three campuses of North West University, a leading university in South Africa.
